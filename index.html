About Me

The vastness of the universe has always captivated me, igniting a lifelong passion for unraveling its mysteries. Have you ever gazed at the stars and wondered what lies beyond our observable universe—or what makes Earth so special? If you do too, welcome to the club. I'm Adhi, an Aerospace Engineer (M.S., University of Colorado Boulder) who bridges engineering and space science. My work focuses on radio science, ionospheric exploration, and the development of embedded instruments and flight software for small satellites. Having contributed to missions like MAXWELL and SWARM-EX, I’ve gained hands-on experience in real-time embedded systems, integration, and spacecraft testing. I’m now seeking opportunities in industry or research where I can apply these skills to push space technology forward.
